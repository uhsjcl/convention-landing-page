CAJCL State Convention

In accordance with guidance from the California Department of Public Health and other state and local authorities, CARCER and CAJCL Convention are canceled and will not be held this year. We regret the necessity of these cancellations and their impact on our extensive preparations throughout the year for Convention as well as on the plans of your delegations. The suspension of these events is, however, in the interest of safety for all those who were to be involved.
